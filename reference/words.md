# Words Data

List of the 1000 most common English headwords (i.e., the dictionary or
encyclopedia entries under which a set of related words appear)
according to the British National Corpus and the Corpus of Contemporary
American English (BNC/COCA).

## Usage

``` r
words
```

## Format

A data frame with 1000 observations of 2 variables

- word:

  an English (head)word

- frequency:

  number of occurrences

## Source

Sourced from <https://www.eapfoundation.com/vocab/general/bnccoca/> on
March 21, 2025
