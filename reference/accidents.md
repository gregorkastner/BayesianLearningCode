# Accidents Data

Seriously injured or killed pedestrians per month in Linz, Austria, from
January 1987 to December 2002.

## Usage

``` r
accidents
```

## Format

A monthly time series object with 192 rows and 4 columns

- children_accidents:

  number of children aged 6 to 10 years seriously injured or killed

- children_exposure:

  estimated number of children exposed

- seniors_accidents:

  senior persons above 65 seriously injured or killed

- seniors_exposure:

  estimated number of seniors exposed
